Dominosa
========

Find the position of the full set of dominoes, which 
is hidden in the grid.  Mark out the 0-0, 0-1, 0-2, 
0-3,...,6-4,6-5,6-6 tiles within the grid so that 
they are all present and there are no duplicates

A-click between two numbers to put a domino over them.
B-click between two numbers to place a reminder.
