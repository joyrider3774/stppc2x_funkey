Slant
=====

Draw a diagonal line in every box so that the 
numbered boxes have that number of diagonal lines 
touching their circles.  

Zero's have to be                  Four's have to be:
diamonds:                          crosses:
/ \                                     \ /
 0                                       4
\ /                                     / \

A-click a square to change it between a blank and 
the two types of diagonal line.
